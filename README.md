# Smart Delivery Operations Analytics System 🚚📊

## Project Overview
This project simulates the role of a Junior Data Analyst in the Business Intelligence team of a fictional food delivery platform. It involves analyzing **10,000+ orders** to solve critical business problems: delivery delays, revenue leakage (cancellations), and customer churn.

## Tech Stack
- **Database:** MySQL (Data Cleaning, Feature Engineering, Advanced Analysis)
- **Visualization:** Power BI (Interactive Dashboards, DAX Measures)
- **Documentation:** Professional Business Insights Report

## Key Business Problems Solved
1. **Delivery Delays:** Identified that bicycle partners in high-traffic zones cause 45%+ of delays.
2. **Order Cancellations:** Found that Cash on Delivery (COD) orders have a 3x higher cancellation rate.
3. **Revenue Growth:** Identified high-AOV cuisine segments (North Indian/Continental) for premium marketing.
4. **Customer Retention:** Identified that 70% of churned users left after a poor 1-star experience.

## Recommendations
- Transition high-traffic routes to **Electric Bikes**.
- Implement **Prepaid-First** incentives to reduce COD cancellations.
- Automated **Service Recovery** credits for users with bad delivery experiences.
